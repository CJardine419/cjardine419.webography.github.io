Source 1

Website URL https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/industry/sustainability/advance-sustainability-ai
Author Microsoft
Abstract Microsoft outlines several strategies for reducing the environmental impact of artificial intelligence through sustainable innovation. These efforts include transitioning to renewable energy sources, improving the efficiency of data centers, and implementing advanced cooling systems to reduce water consumption. Microsoft also emphasizes its goal of becoming “water positive,” meaning it aims to replenish more water than it uses. This perspective highlights the role of major technology companies in leading sustainability efforts and demonstrates how innovation can be directed toward reducing environmental harm. It suggests that while AI contributes to sustainability challenges, it also presents opportunities for meaningful solutions when guided by responsible practices.

Source 2

Website URL https://aimagazine.com/top10/top-10-ai-sustainability-solutions-2025
Author Kristian McCann
Abstract An article from AI Magazine explores how artificial intelligence itself can be used as a tool to support environmental sustainability. The article highlights applications such as optimizing energy use, improving supply chain efficiency, and monitoring environmental changes in real time. These capabilities demonstrate how AI can help reduce resource consumption and support conservation efforts. However, the article also emphasizes that these benefits depend on how AI systems are designed and implemented. This perspective presents AI as both a challenge and a solution, reinforcing the idea that sustainability must be intentionally integrated into technological development to achieve positive outcomes.
